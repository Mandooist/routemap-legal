Account & Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your RouteMap account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

How to Request Deletion

1
Send us an email

Email routemaphelp@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".

2
Include your details

Include the email address or Strava username linked to your RouteMap account so we can identify your data.

3
We confirm and delete

We will confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days of your request.

Email Us to Delete Account

What Gets Deleted

  • Your RouteMap account and profile
  • Your Strava connection and any synced activity data stored by RouteMap
  • Any saved routes, maps, or preferences associated with your account
  • Any purchase entitlement records held by RouteMap

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where required by law or legitimate business purpose:
  • Transaction records required for tax or financial compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics that cannot identify you
  • Data held directly by third-party services (Strava, RevenueCat, Apple, Google) — please refer to their respective deletion policies

Revoke Strava Access

You can independently revoke RouteMap's access to your Strava account at any time via Strava Settings → My Apps. This does not automatically delete your RouteMap account data — please also send us a deletion request using the steps above.
