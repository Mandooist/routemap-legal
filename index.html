Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026

1. About This Policy

RouteMap ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Jason Reeve, based in Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the RouteMap mobile application ("the App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy also addresses the requirements of the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

When you grant location permission, the App accesses your device's GPS coordinates to:

  • Centre the map on your current position
  • Show nearby activities and routes

Location data is processed on-device only. We do not transmit or store your precise location on our servers.

2.2 Photos and Media

When you grant photo library permission, the App may:

  • Save exported map images to your device's photo library
  • Read images you choose to import

We do not upload your photos to any server.

2.3 Usage and Error Data

We may use Sentry (sentry.io) to collect anonymous crash reports and error logs when the App encounters a problem. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, and stack traces. No personal information is included in crash reports.

2.4 Purchase Information

If you purchase a premium subscription or lifetime access, transactions are processed by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). We use RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) to manage entitlements. RevenueCat receives a randomised device identifier and purchase receipt to verify your subscription status. We do not receive or store your credit card details.

2.5 Map Data

The App uses the Google Maps SDK and Google Maps API (Google LLC). When the map is displayed, Google may collect data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We pass your device location to Google only to render the map tile you are viewing.

2.6 No Account Required

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information unless you contact us directly.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Location and photo data is processed entirely on your device and never leaves it.
  • Purchase entitlement data is stored by RevenueCat on servers in the United States.
  • Crash reports are stored by Sentry on servers in the United States or EU.
  • We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect any data we do hold.

6. International Data Transfers

If you are located in Australia, the EU, or the UK, some data may be transferred to third-party providers in the United States. Where this occurs, we rely on those providers' compliance with applicable data transfer frameworks (including EU Standard Contractual Clauses where required).

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data (location, photos): retained only while you use the App; deleted when you uninstall.
  • Crash reports: retained by Sentry for up to 90 days.
  • Purchase records: retained by RevenueCat for the duration required to manage your subscription plus applicable legal retention periods.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to our processing of your personal information, and to withdraw consent at any time.

  • Australian users may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.
  • EU/UK users may contact your local Data Protection Authority.
  • California users (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. You have the right to know what we collect and to request deletion.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
